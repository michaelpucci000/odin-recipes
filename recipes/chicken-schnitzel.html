Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Chicken Schnitzel is a modern take on the traditional German Schnitzel. Historically, Schnitzel is made with veal or pork. Today, we are making a leaner variation with chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Canola Oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat dry chicken breasts and slice into cutlets.
  2. Mix bread crumbs, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Coat in egg, then flour.
  4. Coat in egg again and then bread crumb mixture.
  5. Heat oil in pan and place coated breast inside. Cook for 5 minutes on each side
  6. Remove and serve with wedge of lemon.
