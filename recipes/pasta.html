Pasta

Description

Pasta is the most common dish in Italy. Since the early 20th Century, it has become a staple in the United States as well. Today will be making one of the most popular pasta recipes in the United States, pasta with red sauce.

Ingredients

  • Dried Pasta
  • Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil. Add salt to pot.
  2. Place dried pasta in water and boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain pasta and add olive oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Add jarred sauce and mix.
  5. Grate parmesan cheese on top.
