Pizza

Description

This is a pizza. It is made of dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings. It is a classic Italian dish, but today we will be making the Italian-American version.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Buffala Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar, yeast, and water. Knead flour and water into a ball and place into bowl. Cover and wait 2 hours.
  2. After 2 hours, remove ball and form into a disc.
  3. Take tomato, basil, and olive oil and place into blender. Blend until pureed.
  4. Spread sauce across dough with the back of a spoon.
  5. Top sauce with mozzarella and basil leaves.
  6. Place in center of 450 degree oven and cook for 12 minutes.
